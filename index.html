CodeRev - MathCraft - 2016

This video is part of the MathCraft curriculum offered by CodeRev and used in elementary schools.

Mathcraft uses Minecraft to teach math in a collaborative game environment. The course is applicable for grades 3rd through 7th and is aligned to Common Core math standards.

I recommend you sample the first five minutes of the video get a feel for my editing abilities. The video is paced for voiceover, but there is no audio track currently in this video.

CodeRev - Unity LMS - 2018

This video is part of a weeklong course on the Unity 3D game engine.

The course is targeted toward middle and high school students. It seeks to teach students the fundamentals of the C# programming language and 3d game development. The video is intended to be used as a substitute or supplement for teacher lecture, especially in the case where the teacher is not technically inclined.

The video was captured in Open Broadcast Software and edited on my computer in Sony Vegas. I recording the voiceover myself and I am speaking on the video.

This course is licensed for use in several schools and is currently part of the CodeRev LMS curriculum.
